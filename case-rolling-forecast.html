12-Month Rolling Forecast

Context

Simulated growth-stage company. Data reflects realistic operating patterns.

Problem

  • Static annual budget lost relevance mid-year
  • No scenario visibility for leadership
  • Hiring decisions disconnected from forecast

Approach

  • Driver-based revenue model
  • Fixed vs variable cost structure
  • Headcount tied to growth assumptions
  • Base / Upside / Downside scenarios

Key Insight

Primary revenue risk came from conversion softness rather than pricing pressure.

Recommendation

Pause non-revenue hiring and reforecast monthly.
